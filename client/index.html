About

GuessMyMess.io is a free online multiplayer drawing and guessing pictionary game.

A normal game consists of a few rounds, where every player has to draw their chosen word and others have to guess it to gain points!

How to play

When it's your turn, choose a word and draw it! Everyone else has to guess what you're drawing.

The faster you guess, the more points you get!
